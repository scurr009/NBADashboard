## ðŸŽ“ Key Learnings

### What Makes a Dashboard Beautiful

1. **Whitespace is your friend**
   - Don't fear empty space
   - Gives elements room to breathe
   - Improves readability

2. **System fonts are professional**
   - No loading time
   - Familiar to users
   - Consistent with OS

3. **Subtle beats bold**
   - #FAFAFA better than white
   - #1D1D1F better than black
   - Light borders, not heavy

4. **Consistency creates polish**
   - Use a spacing system (8px grid)
   - Consistent border radius
   - Unified color palette

5. **Less is more**
   - Remove chart junk
   - Minimal gridlines
   - Clear focus
